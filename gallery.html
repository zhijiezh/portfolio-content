I tried to conclude how to take a good photo from my gallery. I got some clues. I am not a master of photography, but I would like to share my thoughts. I believe these records and ideas can help me get better photos.

Beauty is beauty. Photography is not painting, it is the art of capture. Most of the time, you are not creating a beautiful scene, instead, you are trying to capture the wonderful view. No matter if it is a sky full of Chongming lanterns or a sunset in the Caribbean shining on a cruise ship, or the Mackinac Bridge at night. They all are beautiful themselves. As a photographer, I feel extremely satisfied when putting all of them in my camera.

Pure environment. I am not a master, I can not make a chaotic background look beautiful. In a pure environment, I can highlight the main subject, just like the kite in the blue sky.
