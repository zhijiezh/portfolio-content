Places to go

One of my dreams is to travel all over the world and take photographs everywhere. I have taken a lot of photos in Japan. Tokyo, Osaka, Kyoto, Sapporo all left me a deep impression. Unluckily, I didn't got a chance to take photos for Mount Fuji. In United States, I have take photos for cherry blossoms in DC, Route One in California, night view in Chicago and New York. I hope I can got some nice photos for the wonderful Autumn in Michigan.

There are also a lot of places that I haven't been to. I want to go to Iceland to see the Aurora Borealis. I want to see the Niagara Falls. I want to experience the polar day in Antarctica.
